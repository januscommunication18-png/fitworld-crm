# FitCRM - Studio Management Made Simple

## The All-in-One Solution for Fitness Studios

FitCRM is a lightweight, powerful studio management platform built specifically for small Yoga, Pilates, and fitness studios. No bloated features. No enterprise complexity. Just what your studio actually needs.

---

## What is FitCRM?

FitCRM combines two powerful platforms:

### FitNearYou.com (Your Discovery Engine)
A marketplace where fitness customers discover and book classes at nearby studios. Get found by high-intent local customers actively searching for fitness classes.

### FitCRM.net (Your Studio Engine)
A complete studio management SaaS for scheduling, payments, students, and more. Each studio gets their own branded subdomain: `yourstudio.fitcrm.app`

**Together they form a closed loop:** Discovery to booking to long-term retention.
